How It Works

A habit revolution in four steps

Transform complex goals into small, achievable steps.

1
Describe Your Habit
Describe the habit you want to build in a single sentence. Something as simple as "I want to walk for 20 minutes every day" is enough.
2
Let AI Create Your Plan
Our GPT-4 powered engine designs personalized micro-steps for you. Start with just 2 minutes, then gradually increase the difficulty.
3
Complete Your Daily Step
Just one small step each day. As simple as "put your shoes by the door." Sustainable steps that require no motivation.
4
Watch Your Progress
Watch your streak grow, let Mol celebrate with you. Track from widgets, get reminded with notifications.
